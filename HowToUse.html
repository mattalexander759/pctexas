How to use this practice exam

Texas P&C Practice Exam: 25'-26' by Matt

Back to practice exam

The real exam is only 150ish questions, but they are randomly selected more or less from an inventory of 300 to 400 concepts, hence why this practice exam has so many questions.

However it starts off with the more important parts (glossary terms, definitions, policy concepts, etc.).

At the very bottom are the timelines and number-based questions, etc. which seem to be important as well. Symbols are supposedly not very important, but are added at the bottom of "Navigation Sections".

This practice exam is sorted into chunks of 11 questions. At the end of the 11, all the questions answered incorrectly or as "I don't know", will be repeated in a mixed order until corrected.

If you answered correctly but with low confidence, you can click "Repeat question".

At the very end there should be an option to go back and repeat the ones that were missed.

REMINDER: Pay attention to the "insights" for each question.

They provide context and explain roughly WHY, HOW and WHEN you are supposed to apply the concepts.

Simply memorizing specific phrasing is doomed to fail because they will change up the phrasing in the actual exam.
